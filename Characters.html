USER

The main protagonist of the story, is a 17 year old teen who lives with his dad and will progress through the story. Main traits of the user is that it is very lazy and you need to be more productive to pass he's grades

FIONNA

Fionna is a 16 year old girl who loves to do arts and a very extrovert person. The user and her have a mutual connection, she is also the classmate of USER and Josh.

JOSH

Josh is the best friend of the USER where you can get along with the story. He is a 17 year old boy who loves to play games just like user and almost hangs around with the USER. He is also the classmate of USER and Fionna

DAD

The USER's dad is single parent and takes care of USER whole life. The user and the dad were living together for about 13 years since he's mother died.
